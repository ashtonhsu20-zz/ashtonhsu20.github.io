Monthly Comparison of 2012 Illicit Drug Use by People 12 and Older

This project used D3 interactive maps to explore and find correlations between drug usage throughout the U.S. and other factors. We analyzed factors such as region, obesity, crime rate, and median income and attempted to find any relationship between them.

From our graphs, we concluded that obesity and drug usage have a negative relationship. Additionally there is a slight positive relationship with median income and drug usage. We did not find much of a relationship with property crime or violent crime which could be a because the range of crime is very low.
